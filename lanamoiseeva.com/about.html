Hi there, I’m Lana
I am Toronto-based designer specializing in UX/UI and Graphic Design. 
I started my career in an advertising agency and created multiple digital/print advertising products, brand identities, consumer packaging and illustrations. I am passionate about working with people and have 5+ years of experience in teaching Adobe Design Suite. I enjoy creating catchy presentations and sharing my knowledge with others. 
As a UX designer I am inspired by applying a user-centered approach to solve real-world problems and adopting new technologies to make this process more efficient and joyful.
My skills
UX/UI: App/Web Design, UX researches, User Interviews, AB&Card sorting tests, Surveys & Analysis, Usability Testing, Sketches, User Flows, User Personas Development, Wireframing, Information Architecture Development, Light Motion Design, Low and High Fidelity Prototypes;
Graphic Design: Digital/print advertising products, Brand identities, Consumer packaging, Illustrations, Professional photo editing, Simple animation and Illustrated videos.
My tools
Photoshop • Illustrator • Indesign • XD • Figma • InVision • Marvel/POP • After Effects   Lightroom • Overflow • Corel • Microsoft Offices • Google Analytics
