I am happy to share the Aviva Journey app  (2021-22)
Type of project: Aviva Journey - Mobile App (Telematics Insurance)
The goal was to develop a mobile application based on telematics insurance technology. Telematics is also known as usage-based insurance, pay-as-you-drive insurance, or pay-per-mile insurance. The main idea is to provide customers with a discount on their car insurance if they are safer than the typical driver (or if they drive fewer miles than average each year).
Please check out the link for more information  https://www.aviva.ca/en/journey/
